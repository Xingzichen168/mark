The core business of the company is the research and development and manufacturing of FIBC (flexible container bags). We provide reliable, cost-effective, and customizable products to mid- to high-end customers around the world, including conventional container bags and anti-static container bags. Provide highly reliable packaging solutions for industries such as chemical, mineral, food, pharmaceutical, and agriculture.

            

Currently, our manufacturing base is located in Jiangyin, Jiangsu.
